Friday, July 17 • Spice, Rum & Chocolate Adventure
Waterfalls • Chocolate • Rum • Culture
Grenada Shirt Day + Golden Hour Glow

Deluxe Island Tour

Pickup: TBD

Day attire: Wear your Wine About Books Grenada T-shirt with comfortable bottoms. Bring or wear your swimsuit if you want to get in the water.

Discover the culture, flavours, and history of Grenada on a deluxe island one-day tour.

  • Victoria Nutmeg Processing Station, one of the island’s largest nutmeg processing factories.
  • Diamond Chocolate Factory, once a rum distillery and now home of Jouvay chocolate.
  • Belmont Estate, a 17th-century plantation with traditional Grenadian lunch.
  • Panoramic views of Sauteurs from Carib’s Leap.
  • River Antoine Rum Distillery, the oldest water-powered distillery in the Caribbean.
  • Grand Etang rainforest and Mona monkeys.
  • Annandale Falls with the option to dip in the cave/waterfall area.

What to bring: comfortable clothes, swimsuit, biodegradable sunblock, sunglasses, extra cash, and avoid eye-catching jewelry.

After the tour: Free time to relax, shower, and recharge before dinner.

Dinner: 8:00 PM • Restaurant TBD

Evening Theme: Golden Hour Glow. Think bronzes, golds, warm neutrals, sunset colors, glowing skin, and vacation chic.
